M+ Flashcards

M+ Flashcards is a place for students to sharpen their math skills through randomly generated flashcards. Earn or lose points depending on whether you get your question right or wrong, then watch your points score grow. Choose a skill below to get started.

Addition

Earn from 2 to 5 points for each question you answer correctly. Lose from 2 to 5 points for each question you answer incorrectly.

Subtraction

Earn from 5 to 10 points for each question you answer correctly. Lose from 5 to 10 points for each question you answer incorrectly.

Multiplication

Earn from 10 to 20 points for each question you answer correctly. Lose from 10 to 20 points for each question you answer incorrectly.

Division

Earn from 20 to 50 points for each question you answer correctly. Lose from 20 to 50 points for each question you answer incorrectly.
